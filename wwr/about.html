White Water Rafting

Our purpose is to provide a radical escape from the noise of modern life. We use the raw power of the river to wash away daily stress, offering a moving meditation where the only requirement is to be present, breathe, and let the current carry your worries downstream.

History

Whitewater rafting is a recreational outdoor activity that involves navigating a river in an inflatable raft. The history of whitewater rafting can be traced back to the early 19th century when explorers and adventurers began using rafts to navigate rivers for transportation and exploration purposes.

However, it wasn't until the mid-20th century that whitewater rafting emerged as a popular recreational activity. Today, it continues to draw adventurers seeking the thrill of the rapids.
